Advanced Plant Disease Classification

Upload an image of a plant leaf to detect potential diseases

About the Project

This web application uses a deep learning model (ResNet50V2) trained on a comprehensive dataset of plant images to identify various diseases across multiple plant species:

  • Apple Diseases: Including scab, black rot, and cedar apple rust
  • Corn Diseases: Such as common rust, gray leaf spot, and northern leaf blight
  • Grape Diseases: Including black rot, esca, and leaf blight
  • Tomato Diseases: Such as early blight, late blight, and leaf mold
  • And many more: Covering 38 different plant health conditions

The model was trained on high-quality images to ensure accurate detection of various plant diseases, helping farmers and gardeners identify problems early for better crop management.

How to get the best results:

  1. Use clear, well-lit photos of leaves
  2. Focus on the affected area of the plant
  3. Take photos against a neutral background when possible
  4. Include both sides of the leaf if symptoms appear differently
